How To Play

You are the Wifi Wizard! Use your incredible powers of HIGH FREQUENCY ELECTROMAGNETIC RADIATION DETECTION and WIRELESS NETWORK ACCESS POINTS to bring wifi connectivity to the people of the office! Cover as much area as you can with your equipment, then pause and export your score to finish the game.

Controls

Movement

W: Move Forward

S: Move Backwards

A: Move Left

D: Move Right

Access Points

Left Click: Place Torus Access Point

Middle Click: Place Spherical Access Point

Right Click: Place Cone Access Point


Any click: Remove Access Point

UI

P: Pause game

V: Toggle Wifi Visibility
